Who are we?

ANC Brigade is a project of Code for DC, which is a Code for America Brigade. We’re volunteers interested in using technology and open data to improve our civic lives.

We welcome your feedback and ideas: tweet @leahbannon or email us at contactus@ancbrigade.com.

Our goal is to strengthen DC's unique hyper-local level of government called Advisory Neighborhood Commissions (ANCs). This is a work in progress.

Get Involved!

Come to our next meeting if you would like to get involved.

You can also help:

  • Submit ANC documents here or make pull requests on github.
  • Search online for ANC websites and social media and add it to this google doc.
